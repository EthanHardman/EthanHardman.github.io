Services

What Ethan Hardman Audio offers

From full mixdown to subtle vocal polish and production rescue missions – everything is tailored to your track and genre.

Mixing

Full stem mixing for House, DnB, Jungle, UK Garage and other UK electronic styles, plus live/acoustic projects.

  • Clean, balanced, powerful mixes with punch and clarity.
  • Careful attention to low-end control and stereo width.
  • Musical decisions first, technical polish to support them.

Mastering

Streaming-ready stereo mastering that translates on club systems, cars, headphones and everyday speakers.

  • Loudness without killing dynamics.
  • Tonal and spectral balance across playback systems.
  • Masters for streaming platforms and DJ use on request.

Vocal Editing

Polished, natural-sounding vocals that sit comfortably in the mix.

  • Breath and noise cleanup where needed.
  • Subtle tuning and timing correction.
  • Tone shaping and clarity enhancement.

Production Help & WIPs

When you’re too close to the project, an outside ear can make all the difference.

  • Arrangement advice and structure refinement.
  • Sound selection and drum/groove shaping.
  • Fixing or elevating works-in-progress.

All paid services are handled via Fiverr for secure payment and file delivery. Limited free/portfolio work is arranged directly via email when available.

Process

How a typical project runs

  1. 1

    Share your track & goals

    Send a short description, a demo link and what you’d like to improve: clarity, impact, low-end, vocals – anything.

  2. 2

    Project plan & quote

    You’ll get a reply with thoughts on your track, a suggested approach and a quote via Fiverr if it’s a paid project.

  3. 3

    Mix / master & revisions

    Work begins on your stems or stereo mix. Revisions are collaborative – you’re encouraged to give honest feedback.

  4. 4

    Delivery & formats

    Final files are delivered in the formats you need: streaming, DJ-friendly masters, high-res WAV and more.

About

Engineer & musician, not just a technician.

Ethan Hardman is an audio engineer and music producer based in Greater Manchester, UK. He has been studying and working with audio since 2019, specialising in UK electronic music and live/acoustic projects.

With BA (Hons) Music from the University of Leeds, BTEC Music Technology and trumpet performance since 2011, Ethan brings a strong musical ear to every project. The focus is always on musicality, clarity and emotional impact – not just hitting technical targets.

  • Experience mixing House, UK Garage, DnB, Jungle, electronic and live acoustic recordings.
  • Formal instrumental training informs balance, dynamics and phrasing.
  • Approach centred on communication and collaboration with artists.
